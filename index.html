Why Learn Tamil?

Did you know that Tamil is the oldest language in the world still spoken today. It has been found in inscriptions dating back to the 3rd century B.C. If you want to learn a language with a rich culture and history filled with acclaimed poets and litterateurs, Tamil is a great place to start. With over 78 million speakers worldwide it is one of the most well-known Indian languages. Once you have grasped the language, you will be able to communicate with other Tamilians regardless of your background allowing you to have a greater network of friends whom you can surround yourself with!
